Project Overview

BrandWeb Development

Frugal Studio was founded in 2026, born out of a highly successful year of freelancing in 2025. As my client list grew, registering the business became the natural next step.

Frugal Studio is a design and development concept specializing in UX/UI, custom website creation, and digital product solutions. Our expertise spans everything from sleek promotional websites to complex, data-driven dashboards.

Our mission is to empower businesses with digital solutions that are not only visually stunning but also highly functional and user-friendly. We believe in the power of design to transform ideas into impactful digital experiences.

Brand & Identity

The branding leans into a minimalist, clean, and highly functional aesthetic, reflecting the very definition of "frugal" by eliminating unnecessary noise and focusing entirely on what brings value to the user. It pairs a sophisticated, modern color palette with sharp typography to establish an instant sense of tech-forward professionalism and trust.

Scope of Work

For a typical Frugal Studio project, the scope generally encompasses:

  • Discovery & Strategy: Researching user personas, mapping user journeys, and defining the core architecture for both websites and dashboards.
  • UX/UI Design:Crafting high-fidelity wireframes, interactive prototypes, and modern interface designs tailored to the target audience.
  • Web Development:Translating designs into responsive, fast-loading, and SEO-optimized websites.
  • Dashboard Engineering:Designing data visualization tools, intuitive navigation systems, and clean layouts for complex back-end metrics.

Final Outcome

The final delivery results in a seamless digital experience where form perfectly meets function. Clients receive a fully responsive website or dashboard that not only matches their visual identity but actively improves user engagement.

By bridging the gap between clean UX/UI design and robust development, the final product ensures high performance, intuitive navigation, and a powerful online presence that drives measurable results.
